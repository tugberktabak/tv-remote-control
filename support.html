1. Welcome to TV Remote Control Support

Thank you for using TV Remote Control! We’re here to help you with any questions, issues, or feedback regarding the app.

2. How TV Remote Control Works

TV Remote Control allows you to control compatible Smart TVs over your local Wi-Fi network. Make sure your phone and TV are connected to the same network.

The app uses Local Network access to discover and connect to nearby devices. All control commands are processed locally on your device.

3. Common Issues and Solutions

  • TV Not Found: Ensure your TV and phone are on the same Wi-Fi network.
  • Connection Failed: Restart both your TV and your phone, then try reconnecting.
  • Remote Not Responding: Check that your TV supports remote control via network.
  • Permissions Issue: Make sure Local Network permission is enabled in device settings.
  • Subscription Not Active: Tap "Restore Purchases" to sync your subscription.

4. Subscriptions and Purchases

TV Remote Control offers optional premium features via subscription. All payments are securely handled through Apple In-App Purchases.

If your subscription is not recognized, please use the "Restore Purchases" option in the app.
